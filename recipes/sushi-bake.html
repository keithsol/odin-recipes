Sushi Bake

Sushi but baked for your lazy ass

Baked sushi for those who don't know how to roll

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups uncooked sushi rice
  • 1 lb imitation crab meat
  • 2 salmon fillet
  • 1/2 cup Kewpie mayo
  • 1/2 cup Sriracha
  • 1/4 cup cream cheese
  • Nori sheets
  • Furikake

Instructions:

  1. Preheat over to 380 F.
  2. Rinse rice well. Add rice and water to rice cooker, and set to cook.
  3. Airfry salmon fillet to 400 F for 10-12 minutes
  4. Chop up imitation crab meat and cooked salmon in a mixing bowl.
  5. Add mayo and sriracha, stir well to combine.
  6. Dump cooked rice into glass pan, season with furikake and nori sheets.
  7. Spread crab mixture on top of rice and bake for 10 minutes.
  8. Remove from oven and drizzle with mayo and sriracha
  9. Serve hot and eat with nori sheets!
